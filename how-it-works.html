How It Works

How it works

The process of steering you to a top university in India startssoon after you enroll with us based on information we will send you upon request. The enrollment process is quick and simple. The first step is to learn more about you and understand the career direction you plan to follow. And there is more:

Interaction and Communication

We will stay in touch with you using a variety of communication platforms such as email, Whatsapp, phone and Zoom/Google Meet. Call our office during regular working hours including Sundays and access our services without any interruption.

Admissions Collateral

Top universities in India require applicants to submit statements of purpose, essays, scholarship essays, responses to short answer questions, letters of recommendation, and in some cases, writing samples. Our founder, who has several English degrees and has edited thousands of application essays, will personally stay in touch with you and professionally line edit your application collateral. There is no limit to the number of iterations we will edit. We will close the editing process once you and our team is convinced that the application collateral in question meets the highest expectations of your target university.

Online Admissions Interview and Group Discussion

Many of the universities to which you will apply will conduct both interviews and group discussions to identify the most suitable candidates. Based on our extensive database of questions and answers, we will coach you so that you are at your stellar best during both interactions. Our interview and group discussion preparation is designed to present you as the most competitive candidate in the applicant pool.

Applicants applying from outside India

If you are an international applicant applying from outside india, two additional services will be provided to you. The first one is student visa support. Additionally, Alphabet Education will assist you with your travel arrangements including surface transportation if your destination school is located in a city in India without an airport.

Time to Act!

Time is always of the essence when it comes to university admissions. So call us or write to us so that we can chart your upward journey!
