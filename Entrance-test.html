Entrance Test

Top universities in India require all applicants to take an entrance test. In many cases, such as the Vellore Institute of Technology, BITS, and other universities, these entrance tests are specific to the universities with which they are associated. Some universities even accept international standardized tests such as the SAT, ACT, GRE, GMAT, LSAT, and other tests.

We have impaneled India's top test trainers who are available to work with you one-on-one on Zoom/Google Meet and prepare you for a shining finish. Services our impaneled trainers provide include:

  • Demo class
  • Initial diagnostic test to identify gaps in your knowledge
  • Comprehensive training plan
  • Module tests to assess your progress
  • MOC tests to assess your progress
  • Homework assignments
  • Q&A sessions

Before you can enroll with one of our trainers at your expense, we must first determine which universities will be featured on your bucket list so that the entire exercise has a clearly defined focus. Our counselors will monitor your training sessions on an ongoing basis and deliver reports to your parents and us.

Since our trainers are top-flight professionals with national reputations, they tend to stay very busy and are often unavailable close to entrance test dates. So why not initiate the planning phase so that trainers can be appointed for you at the earliest? Please call us or write to us to get the ball rolling!
