Vellore Institute of Technology

Vellore Institute of Technology

Vellore Institute of Technology in Vellore offers a variety of degree programs, so the specific process can vary from one degree program to another. However, here are some common elements:

Entrance Examination

For many degree programs at VIT, especially undergraduate engineering (B.Tech), you'll likely need to take the VIT Engineering Entrance Examination (VITEEE). Other exams might be required for different programs (e.g., VITMEE for M.Tech, CAT/CMAT for MBA). We support admission to all these degree programs.

Application for Admission

We will complete an online application form for you on the VIT website. This usually involves providing personal details, educational background, and program preferences. The application form is rather comprehensive, which is why we should start early and meet all deadlines.

Establishing your Eligibility for your chosen Degree

Each degree program at VIT has specific eligibility criteria, such as academic qualifications and age limits. We will make sure you meet these before we apply for you. Our recommendations will be based on your academic record and the profile information you will provide us.

Final Selection

Admission to VIT is usually based on a combination of factors, including entrance exam scores, academic performance, and sometimes interviews or counseling. As you might have guessed, admission to VIT Vellore is super competitive, which is why our mentorship and guidance will contribute significantly to your VIT admissions efforts.

If you are planning to pursue either an undergraduate science or engineering degree, VIT is your go-to destination. Start your journey early because you will be required to appear for their entrance test and shine with a super score. So why not give us a call and discuss your prospects at VIT? We are standing by to extend a helping hand.
