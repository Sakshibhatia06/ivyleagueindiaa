University of the Fraser Valley Chandigarh Campus

The University of the Fraser Valley (UFV) Chandigarh brings the highest standards of Canadian university education directly to Indian students who wish to pursue Canadian degrees in India. As the only Canadian public university operating a full-fledged campus in India, UFV Chandigarh offers a unique Indo-Canadian experience that combines affordability, quality instruction, flexible transfer options, and global recognition. Launched in 2006, the university is located at the GGD SD College campus in Sector 32-C, Chandigarh.
The University of the Fraser Valley, Canada, was founded in 1974 when it was launched as Fraser Valley College in British Columbia, Canada. Over the decades, UFV has made its mark on the global higher education ecosystem and has evolved from a community college to a highly ranked university. It achieved full university status in 2008. Today, UFV is a fully accredited Canadian public university in Abbotsford, British Columbia, and is recognized internationally for its student-centered approach and commitment to accessible and quality education.
UFV Chandigarh operates within the GGD SD College campus, which is located in the heart of the city, approximately 17 kilometers from Chandigarh International Airport. The campus is part of a highly reputed institutional partnership. The college was founded in 1973, spread over 16.5 acres, and accredited with an A+ grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). UFV Chandigarh employs a unique confluence of international visiting faculty from Canada and highly qualified Indian professors, creating a truly global learning environment. The curriculum is modeled on the Canadian curriculum taught at the parent university campus.

Ranking

UFV Canada is ranked #317 globally by US News and World Report. It has been featured in Times Higher Education World University Rankings multiple times. The university is a fully accredited Canadian public university recognized by the Ministry of Advanced Education, British Columbia. It is a member of Universities Canada (formerly Association of Universities and Colleges of Canada). It has been awarded the EQA brand recognition for quality British Columbia post-secondary education.
GGD SD College has received NAAC A+ Grade accreditation. It is ranked 70th among colleges in India by NIRF 2024. It has also been recognized as 'College with Potential for Excellence' by the University Grants Commission, a Government of India body. It is affiliated with Panjab University, Chandigarh, which awards degrees to those students who complete their degrees entirely at GGD SD in India.

Degrees

Start in India and finish in Canada! UFV Chandigarh delivers four-year Canadian-accredited undergraduate degrees designed around experiential learning and real-world applications. All programs mirror the curriculum offered at UFV's parent campus in British Columbia, ensuring students receive the same quality education regardless of geography.

  • Bachelor of Business Administration
  • Bachelor of Computer Information Systems
  • Bachelor of Arts
  • Management Post-Baccalaureate Certificate
  • Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) - Experiential Business Program
    Duration: 4 years (with transfer options after Year 1, 2, or 3)
    Specializations: accounting, finance, human resources, marketing, operations, organizational studies
    Focus: Real-world business creation, customer engagement, sales execution
  • Bachelor of Computer Information Systems (BCIS) - Experiential Computing Program
    Duration: 4 years (with transfer options after Year 1, 2, or 3)
    Focus: Analytical skills, problem-solving, technical competencies
    Includes AI/ML modules, DevOps tracks, and coding bootcamps
  • Bachelor of Arts (BA) - Experiential Liberal Arts Program
    Duration: 4 years (with transfer options after Year 1, 2, or 3)
    Specializations: Economics, Psychology, Criminal Justice, Media and Communication
    Focus: Critical thinking, curiosity, interdisciplinary skills
  • Management Post-Baccalaureate Certificate (MPBC) - Global Master's Pathway Program

    For students with a three-year bachelor's degree planning to pursue an MBA in Canada

IELTS, however, is required.

    • 1+3 Pathway: Complete Year 1 in Chandigarh, transfer to Canada for Years 2-4

    • 4+0 Option: Complete your entire degree at GGD SD College in Chandigarh and earn an Indian degree

Infrastructure

Students at UFV Chandigarh have access to hostel facilities provided by GGD SD College, offering comfortable accommodation close to campus. The off-campus housing facilities are well-maintained, safe, and secure. There are separate hostels for boys and girls. Full-fledged dining facilities are also available for enrolled students. The on- campus cafeteria is popular among students and serves a wide array of food, snacks, and beverages.
