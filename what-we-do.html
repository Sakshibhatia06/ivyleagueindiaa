What We Do

The process commences soon after you enroll with us. Admission to top universities in India or even outside the country involves a complex set of processes and procedures which we will navigate for you:

🎓
Career Guidance and Mentoring

Using proven and time-tested strategies, we will first conduct several guidance sessions to ensure that your career is headed in the right direction. If you have already decided which career path to pursue, we will surely take that into consideration and validate it.

📖
Profile Review

We will first ask you to share your profile with us including your mark sheets, resume and transcripts to better understand you as an individual and as a professional in the making.

👤
School Shortlisting

The next step is to identify the right top universities in India that align with your career goals. We will even rate them for you!

📝
Preparing your Applications

Once your school shortlist is ready, we will launch your applications for admission and complete the applications for you based on the information we will garner from you through a questionnaire. You will continue to have full access to all your applications and since it will be you who will click the SUBMIT button, you will have full control over your applications. We will also professionally line edit all your application collateral including your SOPs, essays, letters of recommendation and video essays based on the drafts you will provide us.

💻
Scholarships

Many top universities in India offer millions of rupees in scholarships based both on merit and financial need. University scholarship support is a part of our service inventory.

🚀
Final Choice Selection

Once your offer letters arrive, we will help you take a call on which university to attend based on a number of parameters which we will share with you. You will definitely need guidance with this process because you will be spending at least three years of your life if not four at your dream school.

🚀
Facilitation Services

On a case-by-case basis, we will guide you until such a time that you arrive on campus. This service platform includes assistance with accommodation, online registration, documentation and much more. You will always have someone to call whenever you need help with the entire process.

So please go ahead and give us a call so that we can spin the wheels into motion and help you gain admission to a top university in India!
