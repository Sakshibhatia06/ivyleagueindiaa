Resources

Online Resources for you

Resources to take your admissions journey to the next level are abundant on the Internet. However, you will experience serious information overload when you begin trolling the web. To mitigate this circumstance, we offer several solutions:

Determining what type of Resources you need

Since every applicant's needs are different, we will first identify the type of resources you require. Do you need a list of resources to prepare for an entrance test, such as BITSAT? Do you seek information about summer programs abroad? Please let us know, and our team will jump into action promptly.

Online Resources versus Offline Resources

If you need a resource that lists hard copy training materials, we can help you with that as well. Simply specify the focus area, and we will take it from there.

Homework Help for College Students

In the event that you are struggling with homework assignments at your university, we can locate these resources for you as well. While many of these resources are based in countries abroad, they can be of great help since fundamental knowledge remains the same universally.

Signing up for MooCs

MooC stands for Massive Open Online Courses. These are mostly free courses that you can take in a self-paced learning environment, both to learn and to further enhance your profile. MooCs are offered by many top universities abroad, like MIT, Harvard, and Stanford. Ask us and we will research MooCs for you.

Take full advantage of the resources we will recommend to you and enhance your academic profile, your knowledge quotient, and your career prospects! Call us and enroll with us to benefit from a wide array of services we provide that will serve as a launching pad for your career!
