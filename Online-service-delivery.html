Online Consulting

Services delivered by Alphabet Education are rendered exclusively through an online model. This enables us to conveniently address the needs of our applicants regardless of their geographical proximity. Online delivery also allows you to get in touch with us through phone, email, WhatsApp, and Zoom/Google Meet from the convenience of your home or office.

Office Visit

You are always welcome to visit us in Noida, a New Delhi suburb, during regular office hours. We welcome visits by appointment only. Specific discussions regarding your proposed academic journey, however, will take place on Zoom/Google Meet only since not all our counsellors and team members reside in Noida.

Documentation

When you visit us, please bring with you photocopies of your relevant documents, such as your mark sheets, transcripts, degrees, award certificates, appointment letters, and other relevant documents as applicable. This enables us to set up your offline folder in our office if we have not done so already.

As we are sure you can tell, Communication is King when it comes to Alphabet Education. We constantly strive to keep all communication channels open and facilitate your upward bound journey. So why not give us a call today and ask us to tell you how we can propel your academic journey?
