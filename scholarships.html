Scholarships

Scholarships for you

All top universities in India offer scholarships both to incoming and continuing students based on both academic merit and financial need. There are two varients. While some universities will automatically consider you for scholarships if you check the right boxes on your primary application for admission, other universities require applicants to complete a scholarship form. You might also be asked to write a scholarship essay.

Alphabet Education will assist you on all fronts:

  • Identifying scholarships offered by the universities to which you have applied
  • Assistance with the scholarship application form if applicable
  • Editing and proofreading your scholarship essay and bringing it up to specs

Scholarship awards are highly competitive across the board. That being said, our 25 years of rich and highly professional experience will help you reach the finish line.

Third Party Scholarships

In addition to university scholarships and bursaries, there are many non-profits and foundations in India that award scholarships to deserving students. We have a separate process for these awards and will share it with you post enrollment. So why not call us or write to us and discuss your aspirations in strict confidence? We will not spill the beans, we assure you!
