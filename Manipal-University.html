Manipal University

Manipal University

Manipal University operates several campuses worldwide, and we will help you pick the right campus and degree that meets your academic goals. These campuses are located in Manipal, Karnataka, Jaipur, Bangalore, Dubai, Malaysia, Sikkim, and many other parts of the world. The medical school is located in Antigua, which is close to the United States.
Manipal University has a comprehensive application process, primarily centered around the Manipal Entrance Test (MET). Here is a general overview:

Eligibility Check

Before we apply for you, we will ensure that you meet the specific eligibility criteria for your desired degree program. This usually involves academic qualifications and sometimes specific subject requirements. Admission requirements at the main campus, called the Manipal Academy for Higher Education, are slightly higher as opposed to other campuses. It is therefore critical that you perform well on the MET entrance test, for which we will provide you with guidance.

Online Application

Visit the official Manipal University website to learn more about the university and navigate to the admissions section for eligibility details. We will have several recommendations for you while you pick your first choice campus and degree based on the inputs you will provide us.

Registration and Account Creation

We will complete the Manipal online application form for you with accurate personal, academic, and contact details. We will ask you to provide us with the input data through a questionnaire called the Preliminary Assessment Questionnaire or PAQ. Since you will have complete access to the application process, we will ask you to validate the requisite data fields. We will also upload scanned copies of required documents, such as mark sheets, photographs, and identity proof, such as your passport and Aadhar Card. You will then be ready to pay the application fee once all fields are validated both by you and us.

Manipal Entrance Test (MET)

Most degree programs at Manipal University require applicants to appear for the MET. This is a university-level entrance test conducted online at designated centers throughout India and abroad. The MET assesses your knowledge in relevant subjects depending on the degree program for which you have applied. Ask us how we will help you prepare for this and other college entrance tests.

Selection Process

Based on your MET score, you may be shortlisted for further rounds. Some degree programs might have additional selection processes like group discussions or personal interviews, for which we will prep you.

Admission Confirmation

Admitted applicants will receive an offer letter. If you accept the offer and decide to attend Manipal University, assistance will be provided by us to complete all the necessary formalities, such as the payment of tuition fees, accommodation, and other relevant services.

So why not take the plunge and launch your college quest? We are always standing by to help. Write to us, call us, or complete the Inquiry Form and expect a prompt response from us.
