Specially Challenged Applicants

In our ongoing endeavor to support the higher education aspirations of one and all without regard to disability or ethnicity, Alphabet Education welcomes application inquiries from specially challenged applicants who wish to study at a top Indian University. Support we can provide includes but is not limited to:

Identifying barrier free and wheelchair accessible campuses

Our team has identified those top indian university campuses that are accessible for those who need to use a wheel chair on campus. Specific processes and procedures followed by universities make it possible for them to comply with the PWD Act and other Indian statutes.

Interacting with your final choice university to ensure a seamless campus experience

We know which buttons to push and which university officials to tap in the event that you need special accommodation. Since every case is different, we will first make every effort to understand your needs and then take the discussion forward.

Facilitating university scholarship support for disabled applicants

In some instances, universities in India will consider awarding scholarships to specially challenged students in light of their special needs such as ambulatory equipment, braille books, adaptive technology equipment such as laptops equipped with JAWS, NVDA and other screen reading software, tutoring help, and financial assistance to hire readers and scribes. Alphabet Education is committed to exploring avenues for you and helping you obtain the scholarship assistance you deserve.

Facilitating scholarships offered by the Government of India for specially challenged students

Several programs sponsored by the Indian Government support the pursuit of higher education in India for specially challenged students. We will apply for these scholarships for you based on your academic record and financial need.

Dietary accommodation especially for diabetic and allergy-sensitive students

Two top universities in India that we know of provide specially curated dietary programs for those who need them. This category also includes glutin intolerant and lactos intolerant students. we will push the paperwork for you should you choose to pursue your higher education at any one of these universities in India. A healthy, nutritious and safe diet is critical to your educational success.

Special case assistance

Arranging for scribes, extra time during examinations, communicating in braille with those applicants who need this service, and ensuring a 360 degree positive experience are integral components of Alphabet's service delivery mission. Please call us or book a free consultation to discuss your specific circumstances with us.
