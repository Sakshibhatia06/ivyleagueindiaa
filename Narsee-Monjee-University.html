Narsee Monjee University

NMIMS, with its flagship campus in Mumbai and regional campuses all over India, has carved an impressive reputation for itself not only for business, economics, and management education but also for science and technology education, especially AI. Like other top Indian universities, the faculty at NMIMS is first-rate, and admission is super competitive, hence calling for a strong application.
The application process for NMIMS varies depending on the degree program in which you are interested. However, here is a general overview:

Select a Degree Program

If you have decided to study in Mumbai, NMIMS is your first choice. Select a degree based on our counseling and mentoring intervention so that we may recommend the right degree for you, especially given the fact that a single concentration can have multiple variants at the university. NMIMS offers a wide range of programs, including MBA, BBA, Engineering, Law, and more.

Eligibility Check

The university has a stringent admissions process, like many other top universities in India, which we are standing by to check for you. Generally, NMIMS calls for an excellent academic record and a strong cultural profile dotted with social outreach and extra-curricular participation. A powerful statement of purpose is also a component of the application process with which we will assist you.

Entrance Examinations

NMIMS conducts its own entrance examinations for various degree programs, which are conducted multiple times during the year:

  • NMAT by GMAC: For MBA programs
  • NPAT: For undergraduate programs like BBA, B.Com, etc.
  • NLAT: For Law programs
    • You might also need to have appeared for other national-level exams like JEE Main for certain programs, about which we will brief you.
Online Application

Visit the official NMIMS website or the specific website of the NMIMS campus in which you are interested. This will help you prepare your optional list of preferences. Conversely, we will build this superlist for you if you are unsure of where to study for your undergraduate or postgraduate degree. Complete our PAQ questionnaire so that we may gather all the data necessary to prepare your application for admission to NMIMS. We will also upload the documents required by the university to complete all application formalities.

Program-Specific Requirements

Many degree programs at NMIMS, like other top Indian universities, have additional requirements such as a personal statement, essay, or portfolio. We will check the specific degree program details and address them for you.

Final Selection and Admission

Based on your entrance exam scores and other factors such as academic record and program-specific requirements, NMIMS will shortlist you for consideration. Shortlisted applicants will be called for further interaction rounds, which will include a personal interview and group discussion.

Admission Confirmation

If you are selected, you will receive a formal offer of admission through both your NMIMS post-application portal and via email. You will need to accept the offer and pay the tuition fees, including accommodation charges, within the stipulated time frame to confirm your admission. The university offers many off-campus housing options, about which we will brief you at the appropriate time. Our team at Alphabet Education will assist you with the entire process from start to finish.
Admission to NMIMS is based on a combination of factors, including entrance exam scores, academic performance, interviews, and counseling. As you might have rightly guessed, admission to NMIMS is very competitive, especially at the Mumbai campus, which is why our mentorship and guidance will contribute significantly to your NMIMS admission efforts. If you are planning to pursue a degree at NMIMS, you are definitely spot on. We strongly recommend that you start your journey early because you will be required to appear for their entrance test and shine with a super score.
