Who We Are?

Launched in 2001, Alphabet Education has been empowering students across the board with their higher education endeavors. We have many accolades to our credit and many achievements to share!

  • Near 100% admissions success rate
  • Serving applicants both from India and abroad since 2001
  • 100% support and guidance to snag high-paying scholarships
  • End-to-end consulting and mentoring throughout your entire college journey from start to finish

    • Mission Statement

  • To facilitate college admissions for our applicants seamlessly, with clearly defined outcomes
  • To provide all services ethically with no compromise to quality and service reliability
  • To support the career endeavors of our applicants throughout their higher education journey actively and responsibly

The highly experienced and professionally trained team at Alphabet Education understands how admission to top universities in India works. So why not get in touch with us today and take your mission forward? We are open all days, including holidays, to assist you and welcome interaction on the phone, email, WhatsApp, and Zoom/Google Meet.
