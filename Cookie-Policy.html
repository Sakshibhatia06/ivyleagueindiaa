www.IvyLeagueIndia.com Ivy League India ("us", "we", or "our") understands that your privacy is critically important both to you and us. We are, without any reservation, committed to being transparent about the technologies we deploy. This Cookie Policy ("Cookie Policy") explains how and why cookies ( "Cookies") may be stored on and accessed from your device when you use or visit any website or app that posts a link to this Policy (collectively, our "websites"). Please read this Cookie Policy carefully before using our website. This Cookie Policy should be read together with our Privacy Policy and our Terms of Use. In conjunction with each other, they provide a comprehensive overview of our privacy policies and procedures.

By clicking "Accept" when applicable on the Cookie banner, you give us your consent to store and access Cookies and Other tracking technologies as described in this Cookie Policy.

Cookies are small text files, often including unique identifiers, that are launched and sent by web servers to web browsers, and which may then be sent back to the server each time the browser requests a page from the server. Cookies are extremely useful. Cookies enable an internet website to recognize you, log when you visit a particular page, provide a secure connection to a website, and enhance your overall user experience by improving your browsing comfort, and adapting the content of a specific web page to your areas of focus and interest.

www.IvyLeagueIndia.com will leverage information gathered from our Cookies to identify user behavior and to serve content and offers based on your profile, as well as in order to enhance the convenience of the users of our website. We have provided details of the various types of Cookies that are used on our website as well as other relevant and useful information on the Cookies.

www.IvyLeagueIndia.com also gathers data about the pages viewed by our users (e.g. page URLs.) In addition, this website gathers data via Google signals.

www.IvyLeagueIndia.com also uses cookies to understand the usage of the services provided by this website in order to improve our services and to provide services that better meet the interests and needs of our straws purchasing customers. Information gathered includes data on viewing the history of pages on the site, time spent on each page, browser and OS type/version, screen size, referrer, access source, mouse trail, etc. Data on apps, browsers, devices, activity, current location information, etc. is also collected to better serve our straws customers. At any given time, we might utilize the advertising services (display advertising/remarketing/retargeting functions) of ad-serving companies to distribute notices (advertisements) to past visitors to this site when they visit certain pages. In doing so, we use cookies to obtain information on the history of visits to our site. These Cookies are essential to enable you to navigate our website and use its features. These Cookies also allow our Site to remember your previous action within the same browsing session. Cookies also allow our website to remember and record choices you make when you visit our website in order to provide enhanced, more personalized features to you. The types of information collected by Cookies are, language preference, and the geographical region in which you are located. If you do not want Cookies to be placed on your device, we suggest you to set your preferences regarding Cookies on your browser. If you wish to remove previously-stored Cookies, you can manually delete the Cookies at any time through your browser. However, this will not prevent our website from placing further Cookies on your device unless and until you adjust your browser settings.
