Online Degrees

Get a Foreign Degree without leaving your Home Country

Many top universities around the world award their degrees entirely online which you can pursue from the comfort of your home or office without having to leave your home country. Since these undergraduate and postgraduate degrees follow the same curriculum as their offline counterparts taught on campus, they carry the identical weight and prestige you would expect. If an online degree from a foreign university, not mentioned here, interests you, please let us know and we will take care of your admissions process from end to end.
We facilitate these degrees through the following model, which we will customize for you to meet your precise academic and career goals:

  • Identify the right country to suit your geographical preferences
  • Identify the right online degree through a mentoring and advising interaction with you
  • Admit you to the degree of your choice

With extensive experience in the study abroad and overseas education space, we will recommend the right country, degree, and concentration for you based on multiple online interactions with you. You can also attend these universities as an international student and pursue the entire degree on campus with our guidance.

Benefits of pursuing an International Online degree from your Home Country

  • Significantly higher level and quality of guidance, education and training when compared with some universities in your home country
  • A unique combination of instructor-led and Self-paced study
  • Keep your full-time job and earn an online degree simultaneously
  • Continue your studies without interruption while traveling
  • Global recognition of your degree
  • 100% recognized and accredited degrees
  • Flexible schedules
  • No boarding and lodging expenses
  • No visa required
  • Possibility of your completing the final year of your degree on campus, thus qualifying you for a work permit in your destination country

Down Side

While online degrees from foreign universities involve the same achievement levels and recognition as offline, on-campus taught degrees, they usually cost almost the same as offline degrees. We will make every effort to procure a scholarship for you to reduce your tuition costs. Online degrees from universities abroad also do not come with any work permits in your destination country unless you qualify for a final year top-up attendance program.

Benefits of pursuing an Next Steps

  • Schedule an online Zoom interactive session with us
  • Step into the next round to take the discussion forward
  • Activate the admissions process

Once you fully understand how online degrees abroad work, how they will benefit your career trajectory, and have decided to pursue a degree of your choice, please get in touch with us. We have identified the following universities where we can assist you with the admissions process including statements of purpose, essays, short answer questions, video essay, letters of recommendation, optional essay, and the online admissions interview. An eligibility check will follow soon after you have finalized the country and the type of degree you wish to pursue. You can either pursue the entire degree online, opt for a final year top-up program, or pursue your degree in your destination country from day one.
