Profile Enhancement

Attention Parents

If you are contemplating the possibility of sending your child to a top university in India for undergraduate study, an enhanced profile will not only help with the admission process but also qualify your child for a scholarship. Since top universities in India are highly competitive, profile enhancement will undoubtedly benefit you in multiple ways. The Profile Enhancement package, also available as a standalone package, will serve as an asset for children studying in the ninth grade and beyond. Some of the services we will provide your child are listed below:

Constant Communication

Your child will have someone to call or write to and ask as many questions as he or she would like answered about studying in college. Our mentoring team, including our founder, brings over 100 years of cumulative experience to the table. If your child is in middle school, we will ask you to provide him or her with a button phone so that we can constantly communicate with the child without disturbing the parents.

Career Counselling

Career counseling is a part of our service delivery platform. We will conduct a detailed career assessment and recommend career options from a list of 450 options from which your child can choose, since super specialization is indeed the order of the day.

Comprehensive Entrance Test Support

Your child will be briefed well in advance regarding which entrance tests to take and when to take these tests. We will build a detailed planner for your child and make recommendations about the right test, test dates, books, coaching online or offline, and other resources critical to get a really high score on entrance tests.

Online Tutors and Trainers

Our impaneled team of entrance test trainers is well-ranked and first-rate. They will work with your child one-on-one and first identify the gaps and then fill them so that your child's performance touches the 99th percentile.

Action Areas

  • Publishing
  • Portfolio building for art, architecture, graphics, and design students
  • University-based social media profile building
  • Remote internship assistance, where your child will pursue an internship
  • Sports profile enhancement if applicable
  • Leadership
  • Extra-curricular activities
  • Volunteering and community service
  • Organizing campus visits to top Indian universities
  • Many more – we adopt a holistic and customized approach

Resume and Internship Support

We will help your child build a high-quality resume so that your child can apply for online internships under our expert supervision. If your child will be spending the summer vacation in a small city in India and is unable to relocate to a metropolitan city, an online remote internship will be arranged as a part of our package. Applicants in the 12th grade are discouraged from pursuing an internship owing to the possibility of a negative impact on board performance.

Online Publishing

Your child will be encouraged to set up a blog on the Internet and send their blog posts to us for professional line editing and proofreading to achieve a high level of impact on the admissions committees at top Indian universities. Several blog websites set up by our students are available for your review post-enrollment. Your child will be encouraged to submit articles to their local newspapers under our supervision. Line editing and proofreading services will be provided.

College Financial Planning

Finally, we will work with you on the financial planning front so that you are able to plan the funding of your child’s higher education well in advance. As you might already know, higher education at top universities in India can be rather expensive. Please alert us if an educational loan is required.

The Big Story

Every applicant to a top-tier Indian University needs a story, and we will help your child craft this story, usually required in the Statement of Purpose. We will help your child pick one story from a list of stories and experiences that your child will provide us and refine it for submission to universities.

Portfolio

If your child is planning to pursue art, architecture, English, design, fashion, interior design, or music, all of which require stellar portfolio submissions, we will extend a specialist helping hand so that the portfolio that is eventually submitted to final choice universities leaves no stone unturned when it comes to quality, creativity, innovation, and originality. We will provide you with a list of portfolio consultants who, for a reasonable fee, will help your child build a high-impact art, design, or music portfolio.

Very soon, it will be time for you, as a parent, to start thinking about college for your child. Why not give us a call, even if your child is in the ninth grade, and launch the process? Please call us or write to us for a no-obligation interaction on Zoom/Google Meet.
