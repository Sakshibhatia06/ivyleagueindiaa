Study at a top ranked foreign University in India

Want to study at a prestigious foreign university without leaving India? We have several incredible options for you. These are on-campus, in-person, full-time degrees which these universities offer. You will not need to study online.

  • Immurse yourself in a memorable campus experience
  • Receive mentorship and guidance from globally renowned professors who will come to teach you in India from the parent university campus
  • Benefit from the cutting-edge research and innovation the universities will provide you

Operational Campuses

Five top ranked foreign universities have established fully functional and operational campuses in India thus far where you can earn the exact same degree that is awarded on the parent university campus. These campuses in India have state-of-the-art infrastructure and are administered much like the parent university campus. Review the university profiles we have provided below and get in touch with us so that we can first establish your eligibility and then submit your application for admission.

Benefits of earning a Foreign Degree in India

  • Receive an identical degree as the students studying on the parent university campus
  • Earn the degree at a significantly reduced cost with absolutely no compromise to quality
  • Benefit from the liberal scholarship programs the universities offer
  • Qualify for a possible final year top-up on the parent university campus in your final year
  • Complete your English-taught degree in style
  • Make friends with students who will visit India from the parent university campus on student exchange programs
  • Spend a semester on the parent university campus after having spent your first year on the Indian campus
  • Improve your chances significantly of admission to a master's degree on the parent university campus with a full scholarship
  • Reduce your food and nutritional challenges to a minimum while studying in India
  • Stay relatively close to your family and go home for every vacation
