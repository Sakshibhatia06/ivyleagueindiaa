Shiv Nadar University

Shiv Nadar University

One of the top private universities in India with a strong focus on both technology and liberal arts, Shiv Nadar University has emerged over the years as a preferred college destination for many. The application process at Shiv Nadar University generally involves the following steps:

Choose a Degree Program

SNU offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines. Decide, with our help, which degree program interests you so that we can check your eligibility. Some degrees at SNU are of four years duration like many other top universities in India.

Online Application

Please visit the official SNU website and navigate to the admissions pages in order to set up your account. Share the login credentials with us so that we may complete the application for you based on some of the inputs you will be asked to provide us.

Document Upload

In addition to your degrees, mark sheets and transcripts, SNU requires that you upload a passport size photograph which we will upload for you. Please provide us with an impressive-looking photograph because the admissions officer at SNU is bound to look at it!

Entrance Test

For admission to undergraduate degree programs, SNU conducts the Shiv Nadar University Scholastic Aptitude Test (SNUSAT) and Academic Proficiency Test (APT.) The empanelled trainers at Alphabet Education will prepare you for these and other entrance tests should you need this service.

Admissions Interview

The admissions interview carries immense weight for admission to SNU and we will prepare you for the interview if you are shortlisted. Based on your application, entrance examination scores and other elements of your profile, you will be shortlisted for an in-person interview. The interview assesses your personality, communication skills, and overall suitability for the degree program you have chosen.

Admission Confirmation

If you are selected, you will receive an admission offer both through the SNU portal as well as through email. Confirm your admission by paying the required fees within the stipulated time frame. We are standing by to help you not only with the enrollment process but also with the accommodation process.

Because of its close proximity to New Delhi, Shiv Nadar University, located in Noida, is strategically located to enrich your college experience as you transcend academic boundaries and step into an experiential space. So why not call us or write to us and learn more about the mentoring we will provide you? Sign up for a free consultation on Zoom/Google Meet. We assure you that you will not be disappointed!
