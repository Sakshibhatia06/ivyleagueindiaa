Deakin University GIFT City Campus, Gujarat

Deakin University, a highly ranked and prestigious Australian university, officially inaugurated its India campus at Gujarat International Finance Tec-City (GIFT City) on January 10, 2024, marking an iconic milestone on India's higher education canvas. GIFT City is located 21 km from Ahmedabad, thus putting it on the Indian accessible university map.

Ranking

Deakin University Australia's strong global reputation directly benefits students at the India campus. Deakin is ranked 197th globally in the QS World University Rankings 2025, placing it firmly within the top 1% of universities worldwide. The university ranks 201-250 in the Times Higher Education World University Rankings 2025, and 177th in the US News & World Report Best Global Universities 2024. Deakin University is ranked among the top 50 young universities in the world (under 50 years old), demonstrating its rapid rise to global prominence since its establishment in Australia in 1974. The university's parent campus consistently receives a 5+ Star Rating for excellence in teaching, graduate employability, research, and global outlook from the QS Star Rating system. Deakin also ranks among the top 1% globally for business and management, as well as computer science and information systems, according to QS World University Rankings by Subject. This specific subject-area strength is directly relevant to the degree programs offered at GIFT City, ensuring students receive education that is globally recognized in their respective fields. The university's parent campus is accredited by the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business (AACSB), placing it among the elite 6% of business schools globally with international accreditation. It also holds EQUIS and ACS accreditation, further validating the quality of education. These accreditations ensure that graduates possess the skills, knowledge, and experience sought after by employers worldwide. Deakin University has been named Australia's number one public university for overall student experience, maintaining this position for ten consecutive years. The university ranks as the No. 1 university in the Australian state of Victoria for undergraduate employment outcomes. These rankings demonstrate Deakin's commitment not just to academic excellence but also to student satisfaction and career success. It goes without saying that all these accolades, milestones, and achievements trickle down to strategically position the university's GIFT City campus as the preferred choice for postgraduate technical higher education in India.
All degrees offered at Deakin India are approved by TEQSA (Tertiary Education Quality and Standards Agency), Australia's national regulator for higher education. This ensures that the curriculum, teaching quality, and assessment standards are identical to those at Deakin's Australian campuses. Graduates receive degrees that carry the full weight of Deakin University's name without any distinction based on campus location.

Degrees

Deakin University GIFT City Campus has launched two specialized postgraduate degree programs designed to meet India's urgent need for professionals in emerging technology sectors:

  • Master of Cyber Security (Professional)
  • Master of Business Analytics

The Master of Cyber Security degree is a comprehensive two-year degree program equipping students with advanced skills in protecting digital infrastructure, networks, and data systems. With cyber threats escalating globally, this degree prepares graduates for high-demand roles in cybersecurity across government agencies, financial institutions, technology companies, and consulting firms.
The Master of Business Analytics is also a two-year degree program designed to equip students with advanced analytical skills to transform data into actionable business insights. This degree focuses on statistical analysis, predictive modeling, data visualization, and strategic decision-making using innovative analytics tools and technologies. Students who graduate after completing the degree emerge as data-driven professionals capable of solving complex business problems across industries, including finance, retail, healthcare, and technology.
Both degrees feature innovative curricula designed to meet industry needs, incorporating practical learning, industry projects, and work-integrated experiences. The curricula at the GIFT City campus align with Australia's national accreditation body, the Tertiary Education Quality and Standards Agency (TEQSA), ensuring identical standards to those maintained at Deakin University's Australian campuses. Undergraduate degrees have not been launched yet on the GIFT City campus.
According to recent industry reports, the highest package offered at Deakin GIFT City in the 2025 placement cycle reached INR 32+ lakh per annum, demonstrating that the campus is already competing with top universities in India. The average package has been competitive, with most students receiving offers in the INR 12-18 lakh range.
International placements have also materialized, with some students receiving offers in Singapore, Dubai, and Australia, with packages averaging USD 90,000-110,000. Both degree programs are structured with flexibility in mind, incorporating trimester systems that allow intensive focus on subjects. Students benefit from a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical application, with emphasis on emerging technologies including artificial intelligence, machine learning, blockchain, and advanced data analytics. Faculty members from Deakin's Australian campuses regularly visit the India campus for guest lectures, intensive teaching modules, and collaborative research projects, ensuring continuous knowledge exchange. This "fly-in faculty" model complements the resident teaching staff, giving students access to Deakin's global academic community. The curriculum integrates work- integrated learning opportunities, allowing students to apply classroom knowledge in real workplace settings. Students participate in live industry projects, case studies from real companies, and internships that often convert to pre-placement offers. This hands-on approach ensures graduates are not just job-ready but also innovation-ready.

Research

Deakin University brings its strong research culture to the GIFT City campus, encouraging students to engage with cutting-edge developments in their fields. The university's research strengths are particularly impressive in areas such as health, artificial intelligence, sustainability, nanotechnology, and data analytics. Deakin established a joint center focused on nanotechnology and sustainability with The Energy and Resources Institute (TERI), one of India's leading research organizations. This collaboration exemplifies the university's commitment to addressing global challenges through research partnerships. The university continues to foster research collaborations with Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) launched 30 years ago, working on pioneering projects in various technology domains. These long-standing partnerships demonstrate Deakin's integration into India's research ecosystem and commitment to contributing to India's scientific and technological advancement.
At the GIFT City campus, faculty members are actively engaged in research, and students have opportunities to participate in research projects, industry-sponsored studies, and innovation initiatives. This research exposure enriches the educational experience, develops critical thinking skills, and prepares students for research-oriented career paths or further academic pursuits.
During the 30 Years of Impact celebrations in 2024, Deakin University announced a commitment to investing AUD 1 million for strategic initiatives in higher education, sports, and disaster management in India. The university has also committed to assisting the National Institute of Disaster Management in building disaster-resilience architecture in India. These initiatives demonstrate Deakin's ongoing commitment to contributing to India's 360-degree development through research and knowledge exchange.

Scholarships

Deakin University GIFT City has established a comprehensive scholarship program to support talented Indian students. The Deakin GIFT City Meritorious Scholarship offers eight outstanding students (four from each degree program) a 25% waiver on tuition fees for the entire duration of their two-year program. These scholarships are awarded to exceptionally high-achieving students based on rigorous evaluation. The 20% Accelerator scholarship is available for all eligible students to support their education and career aspirations.

Infrastructure

The Deakin University GIFT City campus is strategically positioned in GIFT City, India's first operational greenfield smart city designed to become a global financial and information technology hub. Located in the Gandhinagar district of Gujarat, between the state capital Gandhinagar and the commercial hub Ahmedabad, GIFT City represents India's premier International Financial Services Center (IFSC). The campus occupies purpose-built facilities within this smart business precinct, placing students alongside global and domestic business enterprises. The Deakin GIFT City Campus features world-class facilities designed specifically for modern technology education. The state-of-the-art 25,000-square-foot campus reflects cutting-edge design and construction practices. The innovative design of the campus replicates the quality and standards of Deakin's Australian campuses. The library, for instance, provides both physical and digital resources to support student research and learning. Study spaces and collaborative areas encourage both individual focus and group projects. The campus also features the Clinical Exercise Center supporting student wellness, and various social spaces promoting interaction and community building. Deakin GIFT City has partnered with upscale and high-end accommodation providers to offer housing options near the campus. The recommended accommodation options include Geeta Residency, which offers both boys' and girls' accommodations, and Union Living, which provides girls-only accommodation. The hostel facilities are located off-campus but in proximity to GIFT City, ensuring convenient access for students. Most accommodations offer twin-sharing rooms with modern amenities. The campus cafeteria provides regular meals. This all-inclusive approach helps students and their families budget effectively for the academic year. Hostel facilities typically include 24/7 security with CCTV surveillance, laundry services, library and study spaces, gymnasium facilities, on-site ambulance services, and partnerships with local hospitals for healthcare needs. These comprehensive facilities ensure students can focus on their academic pursuits while maintaining their health and well-being. The off-campus accommodation model allows students to experience independent living while remaining connected to the campus community. Both regular shuttle services and convenient public transportation options connect accommodation facilities to the campus.
