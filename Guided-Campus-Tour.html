Go on a Guided Campus Tour

Guided campus tours are a great way to learn more about your final choice universities in India, experience the ambiance the university will provide you, and meet members of the faculty who will be teaching and mentoring you.

What is a Guided Campus Tour?

A guided campus tour is a short one or two-day visit to your selected university campus where one of the administrators at the university takes you around the campus, explains the university's primary USPs, and introduces you to the university's faculty, students, and staff. A guided campus tour is likely to enhance your chances of being admitted to the university by at least 30% since it clearly indicates what we call "demonstrated interest."

What you can do when on a guided campus tour

  • Validate in person what you have learned and read on the World Wide Web and social media
  • Go beyond the marketing hype and get a real glimpse
  • Clear all your doubts about the university by speaking to administrators, students, staff, and faculty members
  • Check out the living facilities and accommodation arrangements
  • Ensure that the infrastructure meets and exceeds your expectations
  • Visit the on-campus and off-campus cafes, restaurants, food courts, and other dining facilities to get a true sense of the university campus ethos
  • Check out your future cohort by interacting with current students
  • Ask as many questions as you like while on a guided campus tour

You can also visit the library, gymnasium, study halls, and other locations while you are on campus. Since you will be spending up to four years of your life on the campus of your destination university, visiting the university you eventually select makes perfect sense. We will organize the guided campus tours for you once we receive your university superlist from you. You are welcome to visit more than one university campus, depending on your superlist.

We will tell you more about guided campus tours once you finalize the universities you will consider. It is time to get started! Write to us or give us a call so that we can connect online through a Zoom interaction.
