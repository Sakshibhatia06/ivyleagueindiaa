Top Indian Universities For You

Welcome to Ivy League India

The nation's station for admissions to top universities in India! Located in Noida, India, our company was launched in 2001 to address the diverse needs of upward-bound college students and support them during their admissions journey from start to finish.

If you seek full-fledged support, coaching, and mentoring with your admissions process in India, we are the go-to destination!

Programs We Support

  • Bachelor's Degrees
  • Master's Degrees
  • MBA Degrees
  • Doctoral Degrees
  • Postdoctoral Programs

Our Complete Assistance Includes

  • Profile assessment to determine your eligibility at a top Indian university
  • School short-listing
  • School super-listing
  • Application submission
  • Scholarship support
  • Professional editing of SOPs, essays and LORs which all top universities in India require
  • Preparing you for the group discussion and admissions interview
  • Assistance with final school selection
  • Accommodation assistance
  • Travel assistance if you are arriving from outside India
  • In short – guidance and mentoring every step of the way!

Top universities in India are super competitive today — with acceptance rates now less than 5% at most top universities in India. Hence, a super strong application with your right profile elements highlighted needs to be submitted.

Increase your chances of admission to a Tier 1 Indian university by 5X with unlimited hand-holding from Ivy League India! Get in touch with us and propel your career to the dizzy heights of professional success!
