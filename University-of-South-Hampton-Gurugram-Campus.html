The University of South Hampton Gurugram Campus

The University of Southampton inaugurated its India campus in Gurugram (Gurgaon), Haryana, in July 2025, marking a watershed moment in India's higher education landscape. This epic development represents the first time a foreign university has set up a full-fledged campus in India under the University Grants Commission's (Setting up and Operation of Campuses of Foreign Higher Educational Institutions in India) Regulations, 2023. The university is recognized not only by the University Grants Commission, a Government of India body, but also by accreditation agencies from all over the world. The parent university campus, located in the UK with six branches, is a founding member of the prestigious Russell Group, a select group of 24 UK universities recognized for education, research and innovation excellence.

Ranking

The University of Southampton, UK, is ranked among the world's top 100 universities. According to QS World University Rankings 2026, Southampton is ranked 87th worldwide , making it the top UK university operating in South Asia. Southampton is also ranked 115th in the Times Higher Education World University Rankings.

Degrees

The University of Southampton Delhi currently offers six degree programs designed to meet the needs of India's growing global presence.

Undergraduate Degrees

  • BSc (Hons) in Business Management
  • BSc (Hons) in Accounting and Finance
  • BSc (Hons) in Computer Science
  • BSc (Hons) in Economics

All four three-year degrees align completely with internationally acclaimed UK degree benchmarks.

Postgraduate Degrees

  • MSc in Finance
  • MSc in International Management

Both are one-year full-time degrees and commence in early September much like on the parent university campus. The curriculum delivered for all six degrees at the Gurugram campus is equivalent to that taught at the University of Southampton's UK campuses, ensuring students receive the same world-class education regardless of geography.

The Triple Helix Model

A distinctive feature of the University of Southampton Delhi is its implementation of the renowned 'triple helix' model, which integrates education, research, and knowledge exchange with industry and business. This approach strengthens links between these pillars to achieve greater impact and accelerate new ways to tackle the world's most complex problems. The triple helix model positions the campus not merely as an educational institution but also as a dynamic ecosystem where academia, industry, and government collaborate. This creates opportunities for students to engage directly with cutting-edge industries and research initiatives, ensuring their education remains relevant to real-world challenges. The model has been successfully implemented at the University of Southampton's UK campuses and is now being brought to India for the first time.

Scholarships

The University of Southampton Delhi awards several scholarships to support outstanding students and make quality education more accessible. The Delhi Presidential Scholarship represents the most prestigious award, offering full tuition fee coverage for the entire duration of study. One undergraduate student is chosen to receive a 100 percent waiver of tuition fees for three academic years while one postgraduate student will receive full tuition coverage for one academic year. The Future Talent Scholarship aims to assist up to 12 undergraduate and postgraduate students by offering a tuition fee reduction of INR 220,000 for the first year. This competitive scholarship is awarded based on past academic achievements and a strong scholarship application. The Future Talent Scholarship provides need-based financial aid exclusively for academically strong but financially disadvantaged undergraduate students. This scholarship offers awards of up to INR 660,000 per student for one year, with up to eight students receiving this support. In addition to helping you gain admission, Ivy League India will pilot your scholarship application for you at Southampton Delhi and help you qualify for all the various scholarships for which you are eligible.

Infrastructure

The University of Southampton Delhi campus is strategically located within the International Tech Park in Sector 59, Gurugram, one of the most dynamic business and technology hubs in northern India. Spread across 60 acres, the International Tech Park is an IGBC LEED Platinum pre-certified development that exemplifies sustainability and modern infrastructure. The University of Southampton Delhi has partnered to provide premium student living facilities. The accommodation is located at SunCity, a secure gated community in Sector 54, Gurugram, just six kilometers from the campus. The hostel offers various room options including twin deluxe with kitchen, single standard with kitchen, and single standard rooms. Each student has their own bed, wardrobe, and storage space, with study workstations and private en-suite washrooms with toilet and shower in every room. Some rooms feature cooking facilities, and all students have access to larger communal cooking facilities. The accommodation prioritizes student safety with 24/7 security, CCTV surveillance, gated access, and a dedicated secure floor exclusively for female students. Male and female rooms are on separate floors and segregated with controlled access doors to ensure privacy and security. Female guards are stationed outside the women's floor at all times for security. The hostel provides a regular, dedicated shuttle bus service to and from campus on a fixed schedule, ensuring convenient transportation for students. The service operates during key academic hours with secure drop- off and pickup points. Students can also access the accommodation independently via public transport, with the hostel just a 10-minute walk from the nearest Metro station. Meals are provided through a weekly menu catering to a range of regional cuisines and dietary requirements. Breakfast and evening meals are provided at set times seven days a week. The accommodation fosters communal living with vibrant shared spaces on each floor where students can collaborate, relax, and forge lasting friendships.
