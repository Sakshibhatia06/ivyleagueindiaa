Alphabet Education accepts payments through multiple secure platforms

NEFT and RTGS

We will send you our bank details once you are ready to enroll. These payments are usually credited in a few hours once you add us to your NEFT-PAY list.

Bank Wire Transfer

For international payments, we provide all details including SWIFT code to complete your transaction safely.

UPI Transactions

Send payments through UPI. We recommend a small test payment first before the full remittance.

Other Alternatives

Demand drafts, checks, and pay orders are also accepted. We will invoice you with all terms before the transaction.

Regardless of the platform you choose, our team will facilitate your transaction and respond promptly to your confirmation messages.
