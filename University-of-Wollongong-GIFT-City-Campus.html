University of Wollongong GIFT City Campus

The University of Wollongong (UOW), Australia, inaugurated its India campus at Gujarat International Finance Tec- City (GIFT City) on November 15, 2024, marking a significant milestone in the globalization of Indian higher education. GIFT City, India's first operational greenfield smart city, is strategically located between Ahmedabad and Gandhinagar in Gujarat. Spread over 886 acres, GIFT City serves as India's premier International Financial Services Center (IFSC), positioning itself as a global hub for finance, technology, and higher education. The UOW India campus is situated on Level 6 of the Pragya II Building within GIFT City. This location provides students with unparalleled access to leading global financial institutions, multinational corporations, and technology companies that have established operations in GIFT City. Companies including Amazon, Bank of America, Capgemini, Cognizant, Google, HSBC, JP Morgan, EY, PwC, KPMG, LIC, SBI, TCS, and Wipro operate within GIFT City, creating exceptional internship and employment opportunities for students regardless of which GIFT City university you choose as your final choice university. All degrees offered at UOW India are identical to those at the parent institution's home campus in Wollongong, Australia, and hold the same recognition and status, ensuring academic parity and quality standards.

Ranking

The University of Wollongong is ranked 167th globally in the QS World University Rankings 2025, placing it within the top 1% of universities worldwide and ranking it third among universities operating in India. Additionally, UOW ranks among the top 300 universities globally for Computer Science and Information Systems, and among the top 200 for Accounting and Finance according to QS World University Rankings by Subject 2024. The university is ranked 16th in the world among young universities (under 50 years old) according to the Times Higher Education Young University Rankings 2024. UOW ranks 44th in the world for its commitment to the United Nations' sustainable development goals, according to the Times Higher Education Impact Rankings 2024. All the other ranking agencies have rated the university very highly based on the parent university's global reputation as the go-to university in Australia for top-flight higher education. The University is consistently recognized for teaching excellence, ranking as the top-rated public university in Australia for undergraduate student experience, according to the Quality Indicators for Learning and Teaching (QILT). UOW has received 5-star ratings for learner engagement, learning resources, skills development, and teaching quality. This commitment to teaching excellence extends to all UOW campuses globally, including the India campus.
UOW consistently receives a 5+ Star Rating for excellence in teaching, graduate employability, research, and its global outlook from the QS Star Rating system. The university is accredited by the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business (AACSB), placing it among the top 6% of business schools globally with international accreditation. The University's innovative curriculum incorporates the latest technologies, with teaching that prepares students to stay ahead of industry trends. The School of Computing and Information Technology conducts research in areas such as cryptography, artificial intelligence, software engineering, and information systems, and this research excellence directly informs the teaching at the India campus. The university has developed curricula in collaboration with industry, incorporating live projects, mentorship programs, and comprehensive training. This direct industry-education link ensures graduates are workplace-ready from day one, thus facilitating both internships and partnerships alike.
UOW India employs a hybrid faculty model combining fly-in teachers from its Australia and Dubai campuses with locally recruited academic staff. This approach ensures students benefit from UOW's global expertise while also developing local faculty talent. UOW operates campuses in Australia, Dubai, Hong Kong, India, Malaysia, China, and Singapore.
UOW's parent campus in Australia has maintained close ties with over 30 Indian universities for research and educational collaborations spanning more than five decades. These partnerships focus on cutting-edge areas, including software development, advanced medical solutions, molecular and life sciences, advanced manufacturing, cybersecurity, and mining technologies.

Degrees

UOW India offers five specialized postgraduate degrees designed to meet the demands of India's rapidly expanding fintech and technology sectors:

  • Graduate Certificate in Computing
  • Master of Computing (Data Analytics)
  • Graduate Certificate in Financial Technology
  • Master of Financial Technology (FinTech)
  • Master of Financial Technology (Extension)
The Graduate Certificate in Computing can be completed in six months full-time, providing specialized knowledge in computing with a focus on system analysis and project management.
The Master of Computing (Data Analytics) is a comprehensive 18-month degree offering expertise in computing and data analytics, designed to develop highly skilled ICT professionals capable of delivering data-driven insights and intelligence. The degree incorporates the latest technologies, preparing students to stay ahead of industry trends.
The Graduate Certificate in Financial Technology provides an in-depth understanding of how technology is applied to domains such as banking and insurance, and can be completed in six months.
The Master of Financial Technology (FinTech) offers a comprehensive 18-month exploration of the financial technology industry and its impact on the global economy. This is an advanced-level degree for those who wish to enter the finance profession or choose to build on their initial work experience in the finance and fintech industry. The Master of Financial Technology (Extension) is a two-year degree providing a deep dive exploration of FinTech, including courses in web development, AI, system analysis, and project management. The degree is a veritable confluence of financial intelligence and the latest web technologies, including AI.
All degrees offered by UOW India feature stackable, flexible courses in fintech and computer-related fields, with specialized modules in Fintech Foundations, Cyber Security, and AI/ML. This modular structure allows students to customize their learning pathways while ensuring alignment with the fast-evolving needs of the sector. The curriculum at UOW India mirrors exactly what is taught at UOW's Australian campus, ensuring students receive the same world-class education regardless of location. The degrees are designed for diverse learners, from fresh graduates seeking entry into the technology and finance sectors to mid-career professionals looking for upskilling opportunities. Students with computing backgrounds can deepen their specialization, while those without computing backgrounds who are inspired to enter the booming field of technology are equally welcome.

Scholarships

UOW India offers several scholarship programs to make quality education more accessible. The Women Leaders in FinTech and Data Analytics Scholarship offers a 50% reduction in tuition fees for the minimum duration of eligible courses. A minimum of 18 scholarships is available, awarded to top students based on academic excellence. The High-Achiever Scholarship provides either 50% or a 30% tuition fee reduction for students, depending on the academic profile, demonstrating exceptional academic achievement. The Merit Scholarship offers a 30% tuition fee reduction in the first trimester for eligible degrees, with students automatically considered upon application. The Early Bird Scholarship provides a 20% reduction on the first trimester for students who apply early.

Infrastructure

The UOW India campus features state-of-the-art infrastructure built for modern education. The campus is equipped with both online and print library resources, providing students with comprehensive access to academic materials. Study spaces are specifically designed to help students focus on individual work or collaborate with peers. The campus integrates advanced technology throughout, ensuring students have access to cutting-edge tools and resources.

  • Campus Accommodation
    We have compiled a comprehensive list of recommended accommodation providers located near GIFT City. These accommodations are designed specifically for students by private commercial developers. Bliss Serviced Apartments is located near GIFT East Gate within GIFT City itself, providing maximum convenience for students. The facility offers various apartment configurations with modern amenities. Additionally, Januson PG offers multiple locations with varying options for student living. The provider specializes in paying guest accommodations tailored to student needs. Union Living is a women-only accommodation facility located in Kudasan, Gandhinagar. This option provides specialized support and security for female students, addressing specific safety and comfort concerns.
  • Gymnasium
    The Pragya Facility Gym, located conveniently near the campus, offers state-of-the-art equipment and a variety of fitness classes tailored to all levels. The gym's convenient location and supportive environment make it simple for students to prioritize health and fitness amidst their busy academic schedules.
  • Dining
    Food and refreshment options abound, with over six places to grab coffee or snacks near the campus. The new Maple 66 Food County food court has become the fun and food precinct of GIFT City, featuring over 20 international cuisines with a breathtaking skyline view. Numerous cafes, food outlets, and restaurants located in close vicinity to the campus ensure students have diverse dining options.
  • Healthcare
    Healthcare facilities are readily accessible, with a health clinic, hospital, and over three pharmacies open 24 hours, all located near the campus in GIFT City. The availability of premium hospitals and medical facilities ensures students can take care of their health needs conveniently.
